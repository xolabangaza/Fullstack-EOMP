Route Role Description
GET /products showProducts Get all products.
GET /products/:id showProductById Get a single product by its ID.
POST /productsAdd createProduct Create a new product.
PUT /products/:id updateProduct Update an existing product by its ID.
DELETE /products/:id deleteProduct Delete a product by its ID.
PATCH /products/:id patchProduct Update specific fields of a product by its ID.
GET /products/:orderBy showProductsSorted Get all products sorted by price in specified order.
